Round-Robin Algorithm

In this project, I predicted the average amount of salary an MLB team needs to have to gain a certain amount of wins in a season. In this project, I implemented Round-Robin in two different ways, and they are based on priority and (FCFS) First come first served. For the Priority-based Round-Robin algorithm, it arranges the process based on priority and executes the process using the Round-Robin algorithm. Likewise, for the FIFO, the process that was generated first will be completed first. I used C++ to implement this algorithm.
